About

My name is Łukasz Michał Chmielewski and I currently hold the position of Assistant Professor at Masaryk University in Brno, Czech Republic. My primary area of expertise revolves around side-channel analysis (SCA) of public-key cryptosystems. In general, I am also interested in hardware attacks, including fault injection, on real-world devices. In recent years, I have been particularly intrigued by the applications of deep learning to SCA, targeting both symmetric and asymmetric schemes, and I am actively involved in enhancing the capabilities of the side-channel CRoCS lab.
